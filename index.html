Brief History of Space

1957 - Sputnik 1

First Artificial Satellite

Soviets launch the first artificial satellite into orbit, starting the Space Race.

1961 - Yuri Gagarin

First human spaceflight

Soviets launch the first man into space.

1961 - Freedom 7

First piloted spaceflight

First human space mission that landed with the pilot (Alan Shepard) still in the spacecraft.

1968 - Apollo 8

First human-crewed orbit of the Moon

The crew of Apollo 8 were the first humans to enter the gravitational influence of another celestial body.

1969 - Apollo 11

First humans on the Moon

Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin, and Michael Collins complete their successful mission to the Moon.
